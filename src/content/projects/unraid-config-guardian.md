Lightweight tooling to protect Unraid server configurations by keeping repeatable snapshots and human-readable restore notes.

- Focuses on low-touch backups so changes are versioned before risky upgrades or hardware swaps.
- Includes checklists for restoring and validating services after a config revert.
- Built for homelab use; emphasizes clarity over bells and whistles.
